    Nestled in Pune's vibrant Kothrud neighborhood, discover the exquisite residence known as Nesara Tower. Spanning an impressive carpet area ranging from 1,511 to 3,463 sq. ft., Nesara Tower offers a selection of 3BHK and 4BHK flats, each boasting four distinct carpet sections. Strategically located, Nesara provides easy access to Bavdhan, Prabhat Road, Hinjewadi, and more, with travel times of 2 minutes, 20 minutes, and 25 minutes, respectively.
